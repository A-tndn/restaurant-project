Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor & Craft

Born from a love of open-flame cooking and seasonal ingredients, Ember & Oak has redefined fine dining since 2018. Every dish tells a story of tradition, innovation, and the pure joy of gathering around a table.

Our kitchen is guided by simplicity — the best ingredients, treated with respect, elevated by fire. From our hand-built oak-wood oven to our locally sourced produce, every detail matters.
